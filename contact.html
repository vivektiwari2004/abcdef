How to reach G.P. Bhagalpur

The campus is spread in 167 acres flanked by the Ganges. It is 5 hours and 225 kms away by train from the state capital, Patna. It is situated at a distance of nearly 9 Km East to Bhagalpur Railway Station and 20 Km North to Naugachhia Railway Station. The Latitudes and Longitudes are 25.2599ON and 87.0274OE.. Nearly five hrs train journey connects this town from Patna, located at a distance of 225 kms.
